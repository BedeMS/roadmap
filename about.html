Our Goal:

The purpose of this website is to give you a self-taught developer path (curriculum). Sort of like you're own personal bootcamp but on your own pace. The end goal is to make you a Self-taught developer. One of the most challenging parts when starting out is where to begin. A sea of information on where, what, how, why and everything else in between. As a beginner, alone, you'll drown. That's why I have created this Roadmap. It gives you a curriculum you can follow based on your timeline. All the courses provided for you to follow either come from youtube (free) or udemy ($15; they go on sale every 2 weeks). I'm not an instructor, but the courses I recommend are courses I've completed myself. The instructors are all great instructors and are well known in the developer community. So get started with you're own personal roadmap.
