What is Front-End

You will be working with the "client" (front) side of the application. This has to do with how your design and code makes the users interaction with the app enjoyable, smooth and easy. All the content you see on the internet when you scroll and click are probably done by the FE developer.

When a user enters the wrong credit card info, how do you inform the user? Make the form red, display a message or animation.

Ex: The animations, 3D canvas, color transitions.

*Even if you start here, you can go to back-end after. Transition will be easy b/c the concepts are similar.

What is Back-end?

As a user, we're usually creating, deleting, editing or requesting for things all the time on the internet. These interactions are made to look good on the front for the user while the back-end is actually performing these functionalities.

This involves you working behind the scene. If a user tries to log in but gives the wrong password, your work in the back-end will verify the data and send a message to the front letting the user know their password was wrong.

You'll be working on the efficiency of the application and functionality.
You will handle user data, network system, server and more powerful items.

Front + Back = Full Stack

Please understand: while this is amazing, front-end or back-end on their own are large stacks. They are sufficient on their own. You can be a creator or employed with either or. Pick what you think you'll enjoy working on and once you've mastered that then you should move on to the next.

Full stack is definitely doable but requires more time, budget and patience.
